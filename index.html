Welcome to tukar-je!

A simple platform for MMU students who wants to swap rooms with other fellow residents. Whether you're looking for a quieter space, a specific block, or a different room type, you can easily browse available listings based on your preferences. Find rooms based on your preferences, make a request, and get approval from the hostel management. Simple, convenient, and made to improve hostel life for the MMU community!
